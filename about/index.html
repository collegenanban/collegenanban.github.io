About Merit Seat

MeritSeat.in is a student-first platform committed to simplifying college admissions across India. Originally focused on Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions (TNEA), it now embraces a broader mission—empowering aspirants across all Indian universities.

Our Vision

To help students make transparent, confident, and informed admission decisions—where talent, not financial privilege, determines opportunity.

What You Can Do

  • Estimate admission possibilities based on your cutoff and category
  • Filter options by university, district, college, or branch
  • Identify likelihood using clear, color-coded possibility levels
  • Reference previous year admission trends for smarter planning

About the Founder

Dhileephan Ravindran is the founder of MeritSeat.in, driven by the belief that,

Merit-not Money-should decide who gets the seat
This platform is a response to the confusion and inequality in the admissions landscape. Every aspect of this site is thoughtfully crafted to reflect fairness, usability, and a commitment to student empowerment.

MeritSeat.in is more than just a tool-it’s a statement: The Seat should go to those who’ve earned it through Merit.
